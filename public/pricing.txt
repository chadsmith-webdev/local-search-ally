## Two ways to work together

There's a self-serve tool for contractors who want to manage their own SEO,
and a done-for-you managed service for contractors who want Chad to run it.

For owner-operators who want to track and improve their own local SEO.

Includes:
- Full local SEO audit dashboard (GBP, reviews, citations, backlinks, on-page, technical, AI visibility)
- Geo-grid rank tracker (see where you rank across your service area)
- Score history — watch your numbers move over time
- Priority action list — what to fix first, explained in plain language
- 14-day free trial, cancel anytime before trial ends

Sign up at: https://audit.localsearchally.com/signup

For contractors who want Chad to handle their local SEO entirely.

- Google Business Profile optimization and ongoing management
- Citation audit and cleanup (NAP consistency across all major directories)
- On-page local SEO improvements (service pages, title tags, schema)
- Reputation system (review request process, monitoring, response templates)
- Monthly ranking and visibility report
- Direct access to Chad — no account manager in between
- Dashboard access so you can watch the numbers move

Best for: Home service contractors in Northwest Arkansas who want to rank in
the Google Map Pack and get more calls from local search — without spending
time on it themselves.
