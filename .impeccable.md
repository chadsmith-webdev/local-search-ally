# Design Context for Local Search Ally

## Target Audience

- Contractors in Northwest Arkansas (NWA) - plumbers, electricians, HVAC, general contractors
- Small to medium business owners who do great work but struggle with online visibility
- People who are skeptical of marketing "gurus" and want straightforward, honest advice
- Busy professionals who value transparency and no-BS communication

## Use Cases

- Getting found when homeowners search "plumber near me" or "HVAC repair Bentonville"
- Converting website visitors into phone calls
- Building online reputation through reviews and citations
- Understanding why they're losing jobs to less skilled competitors

## Brand Personality/Tone

- Honest and transparent - no hype, no jargon
- Approachable expert - knowledgeable but not arrogant
- Straightforward and practical - focus on results, not buzzwords
- Local and relatable - understands NWA market and contractor challenges
- Accountable and hungry - one-person operation, personally invested in success

## Design Goals

- Build trust through credibility and transparency
- Make complex SEO concepts feel accessible
- Encourage action without pressure
- Reflect the quality and professionalism of the contractor clients
- Stand out from generic "digital marketing" sites

## Technical Constraints

- Next.js 16 with App Router
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Fast loading (SEO critical)
- Accessible for all users
- Easy to maintain and update

## Differentiation

- Personal story and transparency (unlike big agencies)
- Contractor-specific focus (unlike generic SEO companies)
- No contracts, honest assessments
- Built the site as a case study itself
