# Skill: LinkedIn Outreach
Description: Specialized strategies for B2B networking and contractor-focused outreach on LinkedIn.

## Core Principles
1. **The Three-Sentence Rule**: Keep initial connection requests and messages under 4 sentences. Focus on one clear value add.
2. **Context First**: Always reference a specific "trigger" (shared group, mutual connection, or recent post engagement).
3. **Value, Not Pitch**: Offer a resource (like a blog post or case study) before asking for a call.

## Post Formatting Rules
- **Headline (Hook)**: Must stop the scroll. Use a controversial or highly relatable statement.
- **Body**: Use single-line paragraphs for readability on mobile.
- **Lists**: Use emojis (✅, 🚀, 💡) instead of standard bullet points.
- **Hashtags**: Use 3-5 relevant hashtags at the bottom.

## Engagement Windows
- **Peak Times**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday.
- **Ideal Hours**: 8 AM - 10 AM (Local Time).

## Contractor-Specific Triggers
- "I noticed your recent project in [City]..."
- "I saw your company is growing in the [NWA] area..."
- "I'm working with other contractors in [Trade] and noticed a common trend..."

## Scripts
- `linkedin_utils.py`: Validates post length and formatting.
