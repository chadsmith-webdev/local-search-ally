## Key Weaknesses / LSA Opportunities

1. **Runs Google Ads** — LSA doesn't offer PPC. For a trade owner who just wants to rank organically and in the Map Pack, Counselors will push Google Ads as part of the package. LSA's focused approach (SEO + GBP only) avoids this upsell pressure.
2. **Multi-location / team agency** — Bentonville + 2 FL offices. Less locally embedded than LSA (single operator, Siloam Springs).
3. **"Home services" is a category, not a specialization** — They list 9 industries. Nobody on their team is a home service trade specialist. LSA can out-specialize them.
4. **No pricing transparency** — Same gap as all competitors.
5. **No contractor-specific copy** — "Your phone rings" is general. No mention of HVAC seasonality, emergency plumbing calls, Google Maps for "plumber near me," etc.
6. **No contracts unknown** — No mention of contract terms either way.
